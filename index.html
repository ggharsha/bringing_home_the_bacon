"Bringing Home the Bacon"


Help the pig escape from the angry butcher by navigating around the obstacles in its path!

You can control the pig by using the spacebar or up arrow to jump, and the down arrow to duck.

Avoid stray traffic cones and projectiles thrown by the butcher or you'll be sent to the beginning of the level!

Fill up on apples along the way! Your pig will be hungry with all that running... It'll help you get to the next level.

Run through all five levels to bring home the bacon! You can do it!
